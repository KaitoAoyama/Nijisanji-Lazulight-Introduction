Nijisanji LazuLight

Nijisanji is a company made of vtubers (Virtual Streamer). LazuLight is the first wave of vtubers for Nijisanji EN (English Branch) and they debuted on May 16 2021. Each wave of vtubers have a theme for the design of their model. LazuLight's name is influenced by the word lazulite, which is a gem, and the word light. The name is also influenced by their color scheme of green and blue. Their debut song is called Diamond City Lights.
